# Biwenger Stats - Roadmap

Planned features and ideas to enhance the application.

## ðŸš€ High Priority (Gameplay Value)

### 1. Lineup Optimizer

**Goal**: Suggest the best possible starting 5 from the user's squad.

- **Inputs**: Player form, upcoming opponent difficulty, home/away performance.
- **Output**: A suggested lineup with projected points.

### 2. Trade Analyzer

**Goal**: Evaluate potential transfers before they happen.

- **Feature**: Compare two players side-by-side (Form, Fixtures, Value).
- **Metric**: "Trade Score" - is this a good deal long-term?

### 3. Fixture Difficulty Rating (FDR)

**Goal**: Color-coded rating of upcoming opponents.

- **Implementation**: Rate Euroleague teams by defensive strength.
- **Usage**: Display on player cards to help with "Start vs Sit" decisions.

### 4. Transfer ROI

**Goal**: Track who makes the best business decisions.

- **Metrics**: Profit/Loss per player bought and sold.
- **Leaderboard**: "Wolf of Wall Street" ranking for best traders.

### 5. Draft Analysis

**Goal**: Evaluate the initial draft quality (retrospective).

- **Comparison**: Initial squad value/points vs current status.
- **Insight**: Did you win because of a good draft or good management?
